Colophon

Plays
was compiled from plays written between circa 1385 and 1443 by
Zeami Motokiyo.
They were translated from Japanese between 1906 and 1921 by
Frederick Victor Dickins, George Sansom, Ernest Fenollosa, Yone Noguchi, and Arthur Waley.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Robin Whittleton,
and is based on transcriptions produced between 2005 and 2013 by
David Starner, Marlo Dianne, Charles Franks, Henry Flower, and Distributed Proofreaders
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from
various sources.

The cover page is adapted from
Irises,
a painting completed in 1889 by
Vincent van Gogh.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
